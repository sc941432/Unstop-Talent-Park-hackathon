This app is an innovative application designed to assist employers by analyzing resumes and CVs, highlighting candidates who are the best fit for a given position, and filtering out those who are not.

It employs advanced recommendation engine techniques, including K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) and content-based filtering, to perform precise fuzzy matching between job descriptions and multiple resumes.
