As a Software Development Engineer at Intel, you will play a crucial role in developing software that interfaces with and controls cutting-edge semiconductor hardware. You will be involved in various stages of Intel's hardware product lifecycle, from design, development, and validation to integration and optimization. This role requires a blend of strong technical skills, creativity, and a passion for innovation in technology.

Key Responsibilities:

Develop and optimize software systems that control or interact with hardware.
Participate in the design, development, and debugging of operating systems, firmware, and embedded applications.
Collaborate with hardware engineers to define hardware-software interfaces and develop reliable, optimal solutions.
Implement, verify, and optimize performance-critical code across various platforms and operating systems.
Contribute to all phases of the software development lifecycle including research, design, analysis, testing, and integration.
Maintain and document software functionality and guide future software development projects.
Utilize simulation and emulation tools for software validation and performance tuning.
Engage in post-production software support and upgrades.
Qualifications:

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
Demonstrated experience in software development with proficiency in C, C++, or other high-level programming languages.
Understanding of computer architecture, operating systems, and device architectures.
Experience with embedded systems, firmware, or low-level driver development.
Familiarity with hardware description languages such as VHDL or Verilog is a plus.
Ability to diagnose issues and solve complex problems with innovative solutions.
Strong analytical skills and attention to detail.
Preferred Skills:

Experience with multi-threading, real-time systems, and hardware/software debugging.
Knowledge of Intel architectures, peripheral interfaces, driver development, and firmware.
Proficiency in scripting languages such as Python or Perl.
Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
Motivation to stay updated with the latest technology and software trends.
Benefits:

Competitive salary and performance bonuses.
Comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, and vision insurance.
Retirement savings plan with company match.
Professional development and educational assistance.
Flexible work hours with remote working options.
Employee discounts and perks.
Application Process:
Interested applicants should submit a detailed resume and a cover letter highlighting their qualifications and experiences relevant to the role through Intel’s career portal.
